Recipe of pizza

Step1: Spread the pizza sauce on one side of the pizza base.

Step2:Put grated paneer on it.

Step3:Put choped Capsicum on it.

Step4:Now put boiled pasta.

Step5:Mix some pizza sauce and Mayonnaise and put few spoons on the pasta.

Step6:Now cover the pizza with diced cheese.

Step7:Preheat the microwave oven at 220 degree celcius

Step8:Bake the pizza in convection setting for 8 minutes at 220 degree celcius.
